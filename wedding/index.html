About Us

Anh Tran, an Oregonian, and Naka Pilantanakitti, a Kansas gal, met for the first time on their internship together at Intel, Folsom in 2007. Over the course of six months, the two became the best of friends. They returned for a second internship together the following year.
As fate would have it, the two reunited once again in 2009 after they both enrolled at the University of Arizona for graduate school. Anh started his Ph.D. program in Computer Science and Naka pursued her M.S. in Electrical Engineering.
Through graduate school, the two fell in love and as they say, the rest is history ...
