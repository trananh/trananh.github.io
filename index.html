Welcome

I'm Anh Tran, a Ph.D. candidate in the Computer Science department at the University of Arizona (UA). I received my B.S. from Oregon State University in 2009, and my M.S. from UA in 2011.

At the start of my graduate career, I dabbled in all things AI, including research in cognitive architecture, computer vision, robotics, and machine learning. Currently, I focus primarily on natural language processing and information extraction. My dissertation topic involves using information acquired from large collections of text documents to help identify latent scene attributes in videos (VLSA).

Take a look around, something might peak your interest.

Cheers.
