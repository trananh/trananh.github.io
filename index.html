Welcome

I'm Anh Tran, a Ph.D. candidate in the Computer Science department at the University of Arizona. I received my B.S. from Oregon State University in 2009, and my M.S. from UA in 2011.

Early in my graduate career, I dabbled with all things AI, including research in cognitive architecture, computer vision, robotics, and machine learning. Currently, I focus primarily on natural language processing and computer vision. My dissertation topic involves using information acquired from large collections of text documents to help identify latent scene attributes in videos (VLSA).

Take a look around, you might find something you like.

Cheers.
